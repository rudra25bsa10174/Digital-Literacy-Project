Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist-

✔️ Do’s
1) Maintain a professional and respectful tone in all posts and comments.
2) Think before posting—ensure your content reflects your values and personality positively.
3) Use privacy settings wisely to protect personal information.
4) Share accurate and verified information only.
5) Engage constructively—participate in discussions with respect and openness.

❌ Don’ts
1) Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
2) Avoid sharing confidential or sensitive information (yours or others’).
3) Do not engage in online arguments or trolling.
4) Avoid spreading rumors or fake news.
5) Do not overshare personal details that could harm your digital reputation.
